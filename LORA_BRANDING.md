## Hackathon Project Overview

**Lora** is an intelligent health companion app that brings your Apple Health data to life through natural conversations. She's not just a chatbot - Lora understands your health metrics, speaks with you using natural voice, and provides personalized insights to help you understand your wellbeing.

---

## üåü What Makes Lora Special

### 1. **Personalized Health Intelligence**
Lora doesn't just read numbers - she understands them. Ask her about your steps, heart rate, sleep patterns, or overall health, and she'll provide context-aware insights based on your actual data from Apple Health.

### 2. **Natural Voice Conversations**
Lora speaks with you using natural, human-like voices. Choose from 5 different voice personalities and hear your health insights spoken back to you, making the experience feel like talking to a real health companion.

### 3. **Privacy-First Design**
Your health data is sensitive. Lora offers two AI processing options:
- **Cloud AI** (Gemini) - Fast and powerful insights
- **On-Device AI** - 100% private, your data never leaves your phone

### 4. **Persistent Memory**
Lora remembers your conversations. Every chat is automatically saved, so you can track your health journey over time and pick up right where you left off.
